A Glimpse Into My World

With a deep-rooted passion for artificial intelligence and a curious mind, I've ventured into the realms of animation, game development, and computer vision. My journey has been a rich tapestry of learning and creating, from developing immersive game environments in Unreal Engine to harnessing the power of AI for facial recognition systems. Each project has been a stepping stone, further fueling my enthusiasm for the potential of technology to transform our world.

Beyond coding, my creative expression finds its voice in graphic design, typographic artistry, and the dynamic world of audio and video editing. Projects like logo & poster creation, movie filming and intricate video 7 audio edits have allowed me to blend technology with art, creating visually compelling narratives. This interplay of skills underscores my belief in the symbiosis of design and development.

As I look to the future, my appetite for learning and growing remains insatiable. I'm open to exploring new fields, tackling challenges in emerging technologies, or even venture into fields that have yet to be introduced.
